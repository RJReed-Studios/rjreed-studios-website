Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025 · Applies to all RJReed Studios applications

1. Introduction

RJReed Studios ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Crash reports, performance metrics, and feature usage analytics.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Account Data (if applicable): Email address and username if you create an account in our apps.
  • User Content: Any content you create or upload within our apps.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our applications.
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and support requests.
  • To send you technical notices and updates.
  • To monitor and analyze usage trends to improve user experience.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (such as analytics and crash reporting tools) solely to operate our services. These partners are bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party SDKs (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics) which have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at rob@rjreedstudios.com or use the contact form on our website. Requests will be processed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, object to processing, or request portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at rob@rjreedstudios.com
